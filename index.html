About the company

What does the company do / offer?

Defy+ is an athletic clothing and equipment retailer, as well as a guide to get you started on your fitness journey in style. Here you can also find information about different gyms and programs offered in the Vancouver area.

Who is the company to the potential customers?

The company is both a fitness companion and personal stylist in one, giving them the freedom to feel confident in every form of physical activity.

Why might this company entice someone to purchase their product or service?

Defy+ offers a unique, subscription-based service which allows you to choose entire outfit combinations from brands you love and to discover new options and ways to work out in style. The products can also be sorted by the type of activity, such as cardio training, running, weight lifting, hot yoga, and many more, allowing the customer to discover the best options for every kind of movement.

The colour palette features vibrant warm tones to convey energy and liveliness to get the user excited about exploring the website.
