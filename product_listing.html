Puma Defy Mid Black

Product description

This season, dare to be different with the all new Defy Mid Athletic Shoe from Puma! The Defy Mid Athletic Shoe features a revolutionary game-changing design with breathable knit uppers, TPU overlays for durability, unique forefoot buckle strap closure, Rebound foam midsole, and durable rubber outsole with focused traction points for easy movement.

  • Breathable knit upper with sparse TPU overlays for a minimalist look with added durability
  • Sock-like collar for a cozy fit
  • Dual pull loops for easy on
  • Durable forefoot strap with buckle closure offers a secure fit
  • Rebound foam midsole provides shock-absorbing cushioning
  • Durable rubber outsole with traction points to enable effortless multidirectional movement
  • Speckled gum rubber accents provide increased traction
